Our Mission

Fighting illiteracy and poverty across Africa

Why We Exist

RoRo Foundation was established to address the critical challenges of illiteracy and poverty that hinder Africa's development. We believe that by empowering young voices with education and resources, we can break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future for the continent.

Eliminate Illiteracy

Providing access to quality education and literacy programs for children and youth.

Alleviate Poverty

Creating sustainable economic opportunities through skill development and entrepreneurship training.

Amplify Voices

Empowering youth to participate in decision-making processes that affect their communities.

Creating pathways to success for African youth

Our Founders

RoRo Foundation was established by a group of passionate African leaders who believe in the transformative power of education and youth empowerment. Their shared vision and commitment drive our mission to eliminate illiteracy and poverty across the continent.

Our Founding Story

RoRo Foundation was born in 2018 when our founders witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of illiteracy on a remote community in northern Kenya. What started as a single classroom initiative has grown into a pan-African movement impacting thousands of lives across 15 countries.

The name "RoRo" represents our core philosophy: Resilience, Opportunity, Responsibility, and Optimism - values we instill in every young person we serve.
